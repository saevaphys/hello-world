These are the locations, which you analyzed, with results of the annual energy increase for a Single Axis Tracking (SAT) system vs Fixed Plate solar energy system. The percentages displayed include a small effect due to variations in atmospheric turbidity. In general, cities located closer to the equator have a greater benefit from SAT solar energy systems versus Fixed Plate systems, compared with cities located in northern or southern latitudes further from the equator.
