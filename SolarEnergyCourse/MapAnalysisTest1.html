Summary of Clear Sky Results for Year 2020

These are the locations, which you analyzed, for the annual energy increase of a Single Axis Tracking (SAT) system vs Fixed Plate solar energy system. The percentages displayed include a small effect due to variations in atmospheric turbidity. In general, cities located closer to the equator have a greater benefit from SAT solar energy systems versus Fixed Plate systems, compared with cities located in northern or southern latitudes further from the equator.

Note that because some cities are located relatively close to each other, an interactive map is a good way to explore the analysis results.
